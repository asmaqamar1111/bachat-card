Welcome To Bachat Card

The Bachat Card is a digital card specially designed for the Pakistani community, focusing on both saving and earning.

Bachat Card Powered by Zee Marketing

Pakistan's Largest Virtual Card Offering Extra Discounts on Partnered Brands.

Brands can promote their business on the Bachat Card platform through various promotions and discounts on their products. Each Bachat Card comes with a unique 16-digit number. Users can download the app from the Play Store and sign up to access special features.
Earn money from both active and passive sources. To withdraw income, users must first purchase a Bachat Card, after which they can easily perform withdrawals. Benefits include discounts on partnered brands, monthly lucky draws, exclusive gifts, and insurance benefits. Additionally, you can earn Zee Coins by watching ads and convert them into money.

Discounts

Restaurants, garments, hairdressers, salons, tailors, bike mechanics, pharmacies, medical labs, gyms, and many more brands are listed in our application, all offering discounts that users can avail by using the Bachat Card. Here's how: at the time of paying the bill, open the application, select the brand, scan the QR code, and see the discount on your screen. Plus, you can earn 10 Zee Coins that go into your virtual wallet.

Working Income

With the Bachat Card, you can supplement your regular income through various opportunities tailored to your skills and preferences. Here's how: If someone joins the Bachat Card application using your referral code and buys a card, you are on level 1 and can earn money from the user. For example, The card price is 5000, you can earn 20% from that and get 1000 in your wallet.   • Level 4: Earn 2%
  • Level 5: Earn 1%
