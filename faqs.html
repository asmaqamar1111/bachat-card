Frequently Asked Questions

What is Bachat Card?

The Bachat Card is a virtual card that allows you to earn, make money, and save. Brands can promote their businesses on the Bachat Card platform by offering promotions and discounts on their products.

How does Bachat Card work?

Bachat Card works by providing you with access to exclusive discounts, offers, and rewards at partner merchants and businesses. Simply present your Bachat Card during transactions to enjoy the benefits.

How can I get a Bachat Card?

You can get a Bachat Card by signing up for membership through our mobile app. Membership options may vary, so be sure to check the available plans and benefits.

What are the benefits of having a Bachat Card?

Bachat Card offers a variety of benefits, including discounts on shopping, dining, travel, earning opportunities, cashback rewards, insurance coverage, and more, all designed to help you save money and get more out of life.

Is the Bachat Card valid for a lifetime??

No, the Bachat Card is not for a lifetime. Currently, the card's validity is six months. After six months, you need to recharge your account.
